Halakhah 2

<b>The owner of an evil tongue etc.</b> No, but the first mentioned, namely, the sycophant, is worse than the second, namely, the evil tongue; for the sycophant, mentioned first, is the third to be killed, since sycophancy kills three, whereas the second mentioned is but a sneak killing none but himself.<sup class="footnote-marker">*</sup><i class="footnote"> Maimonides maintains that the evil tongue rather than the sycophant kills three. C.</i>
