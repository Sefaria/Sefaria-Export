Paragraph 34

<b>These are the commandments.</b> “No prophet is permitted to add anything new” (Shabbos 104a). However, he is able to bring back a law that was forgotten using the rules of derivation. Similarly, when we say that Eliyahu will clarify it is also by means of the way of the Torah and its foundations. We rely on a prophet for clarifying the reality, just not for the ruling in halachah.
