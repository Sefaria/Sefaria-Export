Verse 6

<b>Son:</b> He is obligated to honor his father, and likewise, a servant, his master. You are called "sons" and "servants." If then I be a father to you, where is the honor due to me?
<b>Saith the Lord to you:</b> It is you, the priests, who despise my name.
<b>Wherein have we despised:</b> That is to say, I knew that you would say, "Wherein have we despised You?" That is how you despised me.
