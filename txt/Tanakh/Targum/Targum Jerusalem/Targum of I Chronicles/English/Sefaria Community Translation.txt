Targum of I Chronicles



Chapter 1

Adam, Shet, Enosh.
Kenan, Mahalal'el, Yared.
Ḥanokh, Metushelaḥ, Lemekh.
Noaḥ, Shem, Ḥam, and Yaphet.
The sons of Yephet: Gomer and Magog and Madai and Yavan and Tuval and Meshekh and Tiras, and the name of their provinces were Africa and Germania and Media and Macedonia and Bithynia and Ossia and Turcia. <i>Alternate 1:</i> ...their provinces were Afkika and Germania and Hamadan and Macedonia and Bithnya and Mysia and Turkey. <b>3</b> <i>Alternate 2:</i> ...their provinces were Germania and Getaea and Hamran and Obyssus and Bithania and Mysia and Turkey.......
And the sons of Gomer were Ashkenaz and Diphath and Togarma, and the name of their provinces were Asia and Hyrcania. <i>Alternate:</i> ...their provinces were Asia and Persia and Barbary.
And the sons of Macedon were Hellas and Tarsus and Italia and Dardania. <i>Alternate:</i> ...And the sons of Greece were Elisha and Hellas and Tarsus and Achzavaia and Dardania, Rhodes and Hamin and Antioch.)
And the sons of Ḥam were Arab and Egypt and Heracleotes and Canaan....
The sons of Arab were Synidae and India and Smadae and Lybia and Zinnae and the sons of Mauritinus (Zamargad) Damargad and Mezag.
And Arab begat Nimrod, who was the chief of those who would be warriors in sin, who shed innocent blood and rebelled [<i>m'rod</i>] before the LORD.
And Egypt begat the Nivtites and the Mariopthites and the Livkites and the Pentaschoinintes.
and the Pentapolites and the Nisiotaites, from whom came the Philistines and the Cappadocians.
And Canaan begat Bothneas, who built Sidon, he was the firstborn of Canaan and of Heth.
And the Jebusites and the Amorites and the Girgashites.
And the Ḥivites and the Iraqis and the Orthusians.
And the Arethusites and the Emesans and the Antiochians.
The sons of Shem were the Elamites and Assyrians and Arpasdaites and Lydians and Arameans and Armasians and Hulians and Gythrians and Mishchaens.
And the Arpasdaeans begat Shalaḥ, and Shelaḥ begat Ever.
And Ever had two sons. The name  of one was Peleg, because in his days the inhabitants of the world were split [<i>itpligu</i>] to their languages, and the name of the other was Yoqtan since in his day the years of humanity began to diminish [<i>itqta'a</i>] from on account of their sins.
And Yoqtan begat Elmodad, who measured and divided the world with ropes, and Shalef, who diverted [<i>sh'laf</i>] the rivers to their borders, and Ḥaṣarmavet, who set the place of the ambushes to slay those who pass by on the road, and Yeraḥ, who set up inns and anyone who came to eat and drink would be given poinson to kill them and take whatever they had.
And Hadoram and Uzal and Dikla.
And Eval and Abimael and Sheva.
And Ophir the 



















...And these are the kings that ruled over the land of Edom before there was a king that ruled over the Israelites: Balaam son of Beor the wicked, who is Laban the Aramaen, who made a pack with the sons of Esau in order to ruin Jacob and his sons, and sought to destroy them, and ruled over Edom, and the name of the city where he reigned from was Dinhava because it was granted [<i>ityhivat</i>] to him freely.

Chapter 3

And these were the sons of David who were born to him in Ḥebron: his firstborn Amnon by Aḥinoam of  Jezreel, the second Daniel aka Khil'ab who looked exactly like his father [<i>kuleih l'abba</i>] by Avigail of Carmel.
The third Avshalom son of Baacha daugther of Talmai king of Geshur. The fourth Adoniyah son of Ḥagit.
The fifth Shefaṭiah by Aviṭal, the sixth Yitr'am by Eglah his wife, who is Michal the daughter of Shaul.
These six were born to in Hebron, and he reigned there seven years and six months, and thirty-tree years he reigned in Jerusalem.
And these were born to him in Jerusalem: Shim'a and Shovav and Natan and Shlomo, the four of Bath-shua, who is Bathsheva the daughter of Amiel.
And Yivḥar and Elishama and Elifeleṭ.
And Nogah and Nefeg and Yafia.
And Elishama and Elyada and Elifeleṭ, nine.
They are all the sons of David, except for the the sons of the concubines, and Tamar their sister.
And the son of Shlomo was R'ḥav'am, Aviyah his son, Asa his son, Yehoshafaṭ his son.
Yoram his son, Aḥizyahu his son, Yoash, his son.
Amaṣiyahu his son, Azariah his son, Yotam his son.
Aḥaz his son, Ḥizqiyahu his son, Menashe his son.
Amon his son, Yoshiahu his son.
And the sons of Yoshiahu: firstborn Yoḥanan, secondborn Y'hoyaqim, thirdborn Ṣidqiyahu, fourthborn Shalum in whose day the kingship of the house of David ended [<i> shlemat </i>].
And the sons of Y'hoyaqim were Y'khoniyah his son, Ṣidqiyah his son.
And the sons of Yekhonya, Issar, Sh'altiel his son.
And Malkiram and F'daya and Shen'aṣar, Y'qamyah, Hoshama, and N'davyah.
And the sons of F'daya: Z'rubavel and Shim'i. and the son of Z'rubavel was Meshulam, and Ḥananiah and Shlomit their sister.
And Ḥashuva and Ohel and Berekhia and Ḥasaria, Yushav-ḥesed — five.
And the sons of Ḥananiah: P'laṭyah and Yesha'ya, and the sons of R'faya, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Ovadiah, the sons of Sh'khanyah. 
And the sons of Sh'khanya: Sh'ma'ya, and the sons of Sh'ma'ya: Ḥaṭush and Yig'al and Bariaḥ and N'aryah and Shafaṭ — six.
And the sons of N'aryah: Ellyo'enai and Ḥizqiya and 'Azriqam — three.
And the sons of Elyo'enai: Hodavyahu and Elyashiv and F'laya and Aquv and Yoḥanan and D'laya and Anani, he is the King Messiah who in the future will be revealed — altogether seven.
