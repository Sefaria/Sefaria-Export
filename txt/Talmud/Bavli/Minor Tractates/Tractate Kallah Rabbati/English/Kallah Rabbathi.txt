Chapter 9

13. Baraitha.  When about to enter the bath-house how should a man act? He says, "May it be Thy will, O Lord my God, that Thou bring my peace and bring me out in peace.  Do Thou save me from this and similar [dangers] in the future and may no mishap befall me; and if it does, may my death be an atonement for all my sins."
